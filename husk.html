Psyllium Husk Sculptures

I've had an ongoing interest in representations and research done into digestive health and practices. I'm interested in ways of exploding and questioning the taboos around digestion and notions of sanitation. I've started to compile a living directory of contemporary art which opens these conversations through form, participation, and performance. Essay forthcoming.
