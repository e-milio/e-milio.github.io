@Berlin(Sophie's Camera)#sophieandemiliogotoberlin

@Berlin(Sophie's Camera)#sophieandemiliogotoberlin is the first “trip” organized by @( )# (At Objective Pound), a conceptual travel agency developed with Sophie Serber in 2015 which sought to facilitate performative excursions, like a hitchhiking trip from Amsterdam to Berlin, in order to produce networks across city lines and a generative space to reconsider what tourism can look like or be in service to. For this first excursion we hitchhiked from Amsterdam to Berlin while wearing matching travel suits we designed that have relevant information to our trip printed on it in case we got lost.
