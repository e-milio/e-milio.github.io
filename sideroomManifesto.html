Sidreoom Manifesto

People People is a “video manifesto” based off of the written version developed by Amal Alhaag and Maria Guggenbichler commissioned for the closing of The Side Room (and its website) – a feminist art space in Amsterdam which was built out of the side room of a brewery called Butcher's Tears. The Side Room still runs its programs like the DJ Workshop for Women in different spaces across Europe.
