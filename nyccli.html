This is the 5th Version of an informational comic book about the benefits of Community Land Trusts in NYC designed with the Education and Outreach Group of the New York City Community Land Initiative. The Comic will be printed on one side of a folding 32 x 40" poster, the other side will have an infographic version of theinformation shared through narrative in the comic book. We have been wrking on this project since August 2014 and have been meeting bi-monthly to share our progress. Stay tuned and visit NYCCLI's Website for more information about their work
