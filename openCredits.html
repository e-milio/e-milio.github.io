Open Credits

2016






Website, projection installation, interactive kiosk

Open Credits is an interactive platform for audiences to engage with notions of authorship and crediting in exhibition and performance contexts. The platform is flexible and can be installed in a variety of arts settings and exhibition spaces. The installation will include a computer set up where participants can write the way in which they were involved; installing work, visiting the space, curating, exhibiting, etc. This content will then be sorted into a standard film credit format and will be projected into the exhibition space in a way that intervenes with the architecture. The first installment of the platform was at the exhbition If They're OK.
